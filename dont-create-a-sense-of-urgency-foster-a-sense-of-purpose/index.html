Don't create a sense of urgency, foster a sense of purpose.

Trying to create a sense of urgency almost always backfires. I lead software teams. Part of the territory is people (CEOs, stakeholders, customers) wishing we could go faster. I'm often asked - " do you think the team has a strong enough sense of urgency?" or the direct "can you make the team feel a stronger sense of urgency?"

Don't create a sense of urgency, foster a sense of purpose.
